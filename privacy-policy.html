Privacy Policy

Naatu Telugu Entertainment App

Effective Date: December 31, 2023

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how QualiPrise collects, uses, and shares information from users of the Naatu Telugu Entertainment app. This app is designed to provide entertainment content without collecting any personal information from users. However, we do use third-party services that may collect certain data.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information from you directly within the app. However, we do use the following third-party services that may collect certain information:

  • YouTube: We embed YouTube videos within the app. When you watch these videos, YouTube may collect information about your device, IP address, location, and viewing history.
  • Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics about the app, such as the number of active users, screen views, and app crashes. This information helps us improve the app's performance and user experience.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports when the app encounters errors. This information helps us identify and fix bugs in the app.
  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect information about your device, IP address, location, and app usage to serve personalized ads.

Log Data

In case of app errors, we collect log data through Firebase Crashlytics, which may include:

  • Device model and operating system
  • App configuration
  • Time and date of app use
  • Error details

Cookies

We do not use cookies directly within the app. However, third-party services like YouTube and Google AdMob may use cookies. You can manage cookie preferences in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
